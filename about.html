Our Story

Roast & Ritual started as a neighborhood counter where curiosity about origin and roast turned into a simple mission: highlight the flavors of each coffee and the hands behind them. We roast in small batches, cup regularly, and build direct relationships with growers.

Plan a visit

Roasting Philosophy

We aim to reveal each lot’s character — not mask it. That means lighter roast profiles for single-origin lots when the terroir shines, and careful development curves when a sweeter, more caramel profile is desired. Our approach emphasizes clarity, balance, and a clean cup.

Direct Sourcing

We partner with small farms and co-ops, prioritizing transparency and stability for growers.

Small Batches

Roasting small batches allows us to profile and adjust for the best flavor expression.

Education

We host cuppings and workshops to help customers understand what they're tasting.
